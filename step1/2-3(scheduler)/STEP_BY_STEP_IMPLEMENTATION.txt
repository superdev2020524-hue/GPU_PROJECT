                    VM Round-Robin Scheduler

Date Started: January 2026
Objective: Prevent VM monopolization while maintaining priority ordering

================================================================================
                            OVERVIEW
================================================================================

What We're Doing:
----------------
Adding round-robin between VMs at the same priority level to prevent
one VM from monopolizing the GPU.

Current Behavior:
-----------------
- VM1 sends: High, High, High
- VM200 sends: High
- Result: VM1 processes all 3, then VM200 (VM1 monopolizes)

New Behavior:
-------------
- VM1 sends: High, High, High
- VM200 sends: High
- Result: VM1 → VM200 → VM1 → VM1 (fair round-robin)
